Our Commitment to Our University Community

The Smart Canteen at Faculty of Technology University of Ruhuna is dedicated to providing students and staff with a convenient, efficient, and high-quality dining experience. We are committed to delivering quick service, fresh and healthy meals, and affordable options that support the campus community’s needs every day convenience, quality, and affordability to households across the nation.

Mission

To make campus dining efficient, accessible, and enjoyable for everyone.

Vision

To become the leading smart dining solution for universities nationwide, promoting convenience and sustainability.

Key Features / Services:

  • Online pre-ordering & menu browsing
  • Cashless payment options
  • Healthy and diverse meal options
  • Notifications for order readiness

Our Journey Begins

Launched in 2025 with a vision to modernize campus dining and make meal ordering faster, easier, and more efficient for students and staff.
